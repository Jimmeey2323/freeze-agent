What this app does

Flow overview

  1. Authenticates server-side with Momence and refreshes tokens before use.
  2. Looks up the member by email and fetches full member details.
  3. Lists active memberships and evaluates freeze eligibility per policy.
  4. Lets the member choose dates and schedules a freeze if allowed.
Heads up

Historical freeze usage is most accurate when the optional history endpoint configuration is enabled on the server.
